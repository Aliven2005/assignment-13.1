Types of Boarding

Name Description Difficulty
Skateboarding It is a four wheel board that is connected to the bottom of the board and only has one point of turning It is a medium difficulty. Necessary requirements are that praticing balance and pushing off with one foot without wobbling
Snowboarding It is a no wheeled board that your feet are attached to It is a medium to hard difficulty. Reason being is that since your attached to the board you can't really bail so that mean if you fall you fall with the board and you can't correct yourself if you fall
Surfing It is a very long board that is pointy in the front and under it has three fins and has a lot of wax on top It is a very hard difficulty. Because it requires balance, knowledge of the waves, and a very flexible to get on the board
